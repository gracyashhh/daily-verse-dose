Be Confident You Are a Christian

The first step you need to take is to be confident you are a Christian. It would be foolish to think you are a Christian when you are not and, as a result, go to hell when you die.

Therefore, it is important to know that a true Christian believes Jesus is God, Jesus died for your sins and was resurrected from the dead, you have repented of your sins, desire to be forgiven and have submitted yourself to Christ. If these statements are true of you, then you are a Cristian.

Begin Reading the Bible â€” Know More About God

The second step you need to take is to read the Bible. It will reveal that God wants a close relationship with you.

You will also discover one very important thing that God wants from you now that you are a Christian. Jeremiah 9:23-24 tells us that He wants you to know Him. We encourage you to first completely read the gospel of John at least three times. When you finish, we encourage you to take up the Bible in one year Challenge.

Confess Your Sins Every Day â€” Keeps The Relationship Fresh

The third step you need to take is to confess your sins every time you sin. Do this at least once a day. Now you may wonder why.

The answer is that when a person becomes a Christian, God forgives all our sins â€” past, present and future sins. Christians are also declared to be as righteous as Christ (2 Corinthians 5:21). That is, God will never find fault with us because of our sins. But we still need to confess our sins daily because we still sin (1 John 1:9).

The fourth step you need to take is to pray every day. God tells us that He enjoys our prayers and wants us to talk with Him.
